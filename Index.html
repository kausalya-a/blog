Aadhi
20 Feb 1990

Hiphop Tamizha is an Indian musical duo consisting of Adhithya "Adhi" Venkatapathy and R. Jeeva. They are pioneers in the Tamil hip-hop scene, blending traditional Tamil music with contemporary hip-hop and electronic beats. Their breakthrough single, "Club le Mabbu le," gained widespread popularity and marked the beginning of their successful career. In addition to music, they have expanded into filmmaking, with Adhi taking on roles as a director and actor. Known for their innovative sound and cultural impact, Hiphop Tamizha continues to influence the Indian music industry.
